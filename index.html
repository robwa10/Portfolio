Greetings from Florida

For this project we wanted something that was retro and modern. Additionally, the client wanted a design that could be used in a variety of ways. So after some brainstorming we came up this design which draws on the classic postcards but has a modern, flat design feel. And since it's based on a postcard guess what the client did? Yep, printed postcards.

Sunset on the Beach

Here the client wanted a design that was simple but expressed the work they would be doing on their mission trip to FL. They were going to be working with the homeless population near Ft Lauderdale. I tried to create a design that drew it's roots from a classic beach shirt as I knew the design was going to be printed on t-shirts. By putting the minimal amount in the scene you recognize they are on the beach but your attention is drawn to the two figures that seemingly don't belong in the scene.

  • Client: Canterbury Student Ministry
  • Date: June 2017
  • Service: Graphic Design
